About Me

I am a Senior Lecturer (Associate Professor) at Macquarie University and formerly a Research Scientist at Data61, CSIRO. I am a member of the Information Security and Privacy Research Group at Macquarie University. My research interests are in applied cryptography, cryptography for private data processing, differential privacy, user authentication, and privacy and security in machine learning.
